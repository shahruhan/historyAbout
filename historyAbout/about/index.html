Wives of the Prophet Muhammad

The Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him, is considered the best of the prophets and their last one. He is a special to Allah with many characteristics that set him apart from others, including marrying more than four women; He married eleven wives of which God honored by saying: ((The Prophet is first among the believers of themselves and his wives are their mothers)).

  • Khadija bint Khuwaylid al-Qurqisiyyah al-Asadiyah

    The prophet married her before the prophecy. She was 40 years old, and he did not marry any other woman until she died. She is the mother of his children except Ibrahim. She was the one who aided him in the prophecy, and she struggled with him and favored him by herself and her money. She died three years before emigration and the prophet was deeply saddened by her death.

  • Suda bint Zumat al-Qurashya

    The prophet married her days after Khadija’s death. She is the one who gave her day to Aisha, and she was the oldest of the prophet’s wives. Suda died at the end of Omar Ibn Al-Khattab Khilafah.

  • Aisha bint Abi Bakr Al-Siddiq

    She was nicknamed Umm Abdullah, although she had no children. The prophet married her in Shawwal in the first year of emigration when she was nine years old. And she was the one whom the men of the Ifk threw into adultery, and God brought her innocence down from above seven heavens, and she was the most beloved wife of the prophet (Peace and blessing be upon him). The companions used to ask her for Fiqh questions. She died at the 57th of Immigration (Higrah).

  • Hafsa bint Umar bin Al-Khattab

    The prophet married her in the second year of Higrah after Badr war.

  • Zainab bint Khuzayma bin al-Harith al-Qaysiyah

    The prophet married her in the third year of Higrah (immigration) She died two months after the marriage of the Prophet.

  • Umm Salama, Hind bint Umayya al-Qurqishiyah al-Makhzoumi

    The prophet married her in the fourth year of Immigration The last one from the prophet’s wives who died. She died at the year (61) of immigration.

  • Zainab bint Jahsh

    She was the daughter of his aunt Umaima The prophet married her after the order from Allah when he said “after Zaid married her, we gave her to you to marry her“ and thus she was proud of that She was first with Zeid, the son of Harithah, and was adopted by the Messenger of God, Peace be upon Him. When Zeid divorced her The prophet married her she died of a year of twenty-one after immigration.

  • Juwayriya bint al-Harith

    She was married by the Prophet after the invasion of Bani Al-Mustalak, at year number 5 after immigration and the prophet was about 58 years old.

  • Rehana bint Zayd bin Amr bin Khaniqa

    She was married by the Prophet after the invasion of the Banu Qurayza, in the year number 5 after immegration.

  • Um Habiba bint Abi Sufyan

    She was married by the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) in the year of seven after immegration, and he was about 59-61 years old.

  • Safia bint Hayya bin Khattab

    She was married to the Prophet after the invasion of Khyber and at 7th of immegration.

  • Maymuna bint al-Harith al-Hilaliyah

    she was the last one that the prophet Peace and blessings be upon him married her, he married her at Zu-Al Qiada month in year number 7 after immegration.
